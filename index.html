ABOUT

Aerophilia is a National level Technical fest, conducted for students aspiring to be extraordinary. It’s a 2-day event spanning the weekend with an exciting competition involving Aeromodelling, Drone race, RC Bot events, and all things out of this world! There are also fun-filled activities and Hackathons. The motive behind organizing such an event is to spread and teach the young talented minds to explore new areas of technology and aeromodelling to foster the nation's development.

Sahyadri College of Engineering and Management is one of the premier technological institutions inculcating quality and value-based education through the innovative teaching-learning process. The In-house Industries, Startups, and Launchpads are the mainstay of the College.
Challengers is a student-oriented and student-driven open workspace. We participate in the International level, National level, and inter-college events/competitions and explore many domains such as Robotics, Coding, CAD, and Aeromodelling. We organize events and workshops where we learn to manage and communicate. The goal is to develop and create an environment where an individual can grow tremendously in all aspects.
